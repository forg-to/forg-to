# forg.to

**Share what you're working on, ship updates, launch projects, and connect with people who actually get it.**

forg.to gives you a profile that actually represents what you do and not just where you worked.

Connect GitHub, LeetCode, dev.to, Medium, YouTube, Dribbble and more. Every commit, streak, article, and project update flows into a single URL at `forg.to/@yourname`. 

Your project history is a timestamped log, not a screenshot. Your building process is visible, not buried across 6 different platforms.

---

**What we're building**

- **Developer profiles** that aggregate your work from every platform you use
- **Creator Studio** — write once, post to X, LinkedIn, and Bluesky simultaneously
- **GitHub integration** — your PRs become draft social posts automatically
- **Launchpad** — weekly indie product launches with real builder feedback
- **Project tracker** — every update, every status change, permanently logged
